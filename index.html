Must visit places : my experience

Bangalore

I had visited on 16th July, 2016.

Bangalore is known as the "Garden City of India" because of its greenery, broad streets and the presence of many public parks, such as Lal Bagh and Cubbon Park. Bangalore is sometimes called as the "Pub Capital of India" and the "Rock/Metal Capital of India" because of its underground music scene and it is one of the premier places to hold international rock concerts. The diversity of cuisine is reflective of the social and economic diversity of Bangalore. In May 2012, Lonely Planet ranked Bangalore third among the world's top ten cities to visit.



Lucknow

I Had Visited On 3rd July, 2019.

The journey to Lucknow had a different feel about it. The tiny lights visible from the plane were as if to welcome us! We grinned. Luck - now, as it's called '𝘕𝘢𝘸𝘢𝘣𝘰 𝘬𝘢 𝘴𝘩𝘢𝘩𝘢𝘳' which literally means City of Nobleperson or Highstatus. It is indeed rich in culture and tradition and the street food is worth tasting. The numerous monuments including Bhool Bhoolaiya (Maze), Ghanta Ghar, Skyline are once - in - a - lifetime experiences. Lucknow is known for embroidery works including chikankari, zari, zardozi, kamdani and gota making (gold lace weaving).



Delhi

I Had Visited On 27th October, 2021.

Goodness, I still remember our visit to Delhi, what an amazing trip! Delhi Haat, Cannaught Place, Red Fort, Humayun's Tomb, Kidzania, India Gate...glorious! The best way to explore each and every one of these wonders is to hire a city guide. It was a real adventure with an unforgettable experience which apart from being intellectually rewarding gave us a glimpse of our country's proud history.

news about environment

  • Indian scientists develop 'capsules' to curb crop burning

    The Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI), Pusa, Delhi has developed a decomposer capsule, which could be converted into a liquid solution and sprayed on fields with crop stubble. The solution, once sprayed on the fields, will decompose the stubble, which can be used by farmers to treat the fields.
